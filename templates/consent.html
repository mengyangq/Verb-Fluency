Thank you for accepting the HIT. You have been invited to take part in a research study named Verbal Fluency.

Please read this consent agreement carefully before deciding whether to participate in this study.


Consent Form

Purpose of the research:

The purpose of this study is to investigate how you search your memory when you are asked to produce words according to a specified criterion. We would like to see how memory search patterns are similar or different across different categories (e.g., nouns vs. verbs) and across different ages. The results will help us better understand how different words are stored and accessed in our mind, and serve as a basis for exploring memory search deficits of words in people with mild cognitive impairment, early Alzheimer's disease, or other neurodegenerative disorders in future studies.

Eligibility:

A. Please ONLY participate ONCE. DO NOT participate in this HIT if you have previously done it. If you have already participated, your work will be rejected (affecting your worker rating) and you WILL NOT be paid. (You can find out if you've participated previously by checking your account settings or qualifications.)

B. Please ONLY participate in this study if you are a native speaker of American English. If American English is not your first language, you cannot participate. If you have a non-American accent we will NOT be able to pay you. For example, British or Indian English is NOT permissible for this experiment.

C. Please ONLY participate in this study if you are 18 years of age or older.

What you will do in this research:

You will do three verbal fluency tasks. Each will be one minute long. In each task, you will be ask to produce as many words as possible that conform to a specified criterion. Please follow the instructions, and RECORD your response with your microphone.

The study should take no more than 10 minutes.

Risks and benefits:

There are no known risks associated with your participation in this research beyond those of everyday life. Although you will receive no direct benefits, this research may help the investigator understand how people organise and access words in their mind.

Compensation:

You will be paid $1.00 to participate in the study.

Confidentiality:

The data you provide will be anonymous. That is, it is recorded such that not even the person collecting and storing it would ever be able to associate your data with your identity.

Participation and withdrawal:

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time without penalty or loss of benefit to which you may otherwise be entitled.
